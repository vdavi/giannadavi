Welcome to Biology for Kids!

Explore the wonders of plants, animals, and the human body in a fun and engaging way.

📚 What is Biology?

Biology is the scientific study of living organisms and their origins. It explores the structure, function, growth, origin, evolution, and distribution of all living things.

🔬 The Building Blocks of Life!

All living things are made up of cells! These tiny units perform vital functions like creating energy, growing, and reproducing.

🌱 Plants

Plants are living organisms that produce their own food using sunlight, water, and air through a process called photosynthesis.

🐾 Animals

Unlike plants, animals need to consume other organisms to obtain energy. They can move around and live in a variety of environments.

🌿 Plants vs 🦁 Animals

Plants have cell walls, chloroplasts, and large vacuoles, while animals have organelles like lysosomes for waste disposal.

🌎 Ecosystems & Energy Exchange

An ecosystem includes all living and non-living things in an area and how they interact. The food chain illustrates how energy moves between organisms.

🧬 Genetics

Your traits—like eye color or height—are determined by the genetic instructions passed down from your parents.

🐵 Evolution

Evolution explains how species change over time. Natural selection helps organisms with advantageous traits survive and reproduce.
